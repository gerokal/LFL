Privacy Policy

Introduction

London Futsal League ("LFL", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website (londonfutsalleague.com) and our services.

Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data through our registration and contact forms:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth (solo registration only)
  • Postcode / area
  • Team information (team registration only)
  • Messages submitted via the contact form

How We Use Your Data

The data shared with us through this website will be used ONLY for the management of your attendance with LFL futsal teams and futsal casual games in London. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Register you as a player or team in the league
  • Contact you about league schedules, fixtures, and updates
  • Match solo players with available teams
  • Respond to enquiries submitted via the contact form

Your data is not shared with third parties except for the futsal teams participating in the LFL, as necessary for league operations.

Data Storage

Form submissions are processed via Google Forms and stored in Google Sheets. Google's privacy policy and data processing terms apply to the storage of this data. We do not store your data on any other third-party platforms.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies for analytics purposes (Google Analytics) and to manage cookie consent (CookieYes). Analytics cookies are only activated after you provide consent via the cookie banner. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through the cookie settings on our website.

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@londonfutsalleague.com.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your personal data, please contact us:

Email: contact@londonfutsalleague.com

Or use our contact form.
